Zombie Plague 5.0 Leo_BH Editing is fork of original Zombie Plague 5.0 Mod. Based on Zombie Plague 5.0.8a.

Key principles to developing this fork: 
- More comfortable for the eyes. Less flashing, strobing, blinking, flickering lights. 
- More FPS. Improved game perfomance. Less dynamic lighting. 
- More customization. More variables. More customization. More gameplay balance tweaks. 
- Keep original gameplay and content.
